^[A-Z]
Words with first letter capped

^[^A-Za-z]*[0-9]+[^A-Za-z]*
Number, possibly with some exotic characters accompanying (hyphens, minus, dash, slash, etc.)

^[A-Za-z]{1,2}$
Words with alphabetical letters only, length up to 2

^[A-Za-z]{1,3}$
Words with alphabetical letters only, length up to 3

^[A-Za-z]{1,4}$
Words with alphabetical letters only, length up to 4

^[A-Za-z]{1,5}$
Words with alphabetical letters only, length up to 5

^[A-Za-z]{1,6}$
Words with alphabetical letters only, length up to 6

^[A-Za-z]{1,7}$
Words with alphabetical letters only, length up to 7

^[A-Za-z]{1,8}$
Words with alphabetical letters only, length up to 8

^[A-Za-z]+ity$
Words ended with -ity suffix

^[A-Za-z]+al$
Words ended with -al suffix

^[A-Za-z]+s$
Words ended with -s suffix

^[A-Za-z]+ed$
Words ended with -ed suffix

^[A-Za-z]+er$
Words ended with -er suffix

^[A-Za-z]+est$
Words ended with -est suffix

^[Ii]n[A-Za-z]+$
Words started with in- prefix

^[Ii]r[A-Za-z]+$
Words started with ir- prefix

^[Ii]m[A-Za-z]+$
Words started with im- prefix

^[Uu]n[A-Za-z]+$
Words started with un- prefix

^[D]e[A-Za-z]+$
Words started with de- prefix

^[D]is[A-Za-z]+$
Words started with dis- prefix

^[A-Za-z]+-[A-Za-z]+$
Words with a single hyphen

^[A-Za-z]+-(one|two|three|four|five|six|seven|eight|nine)$
Words with a single hyphen followed by a number (possibly a two-digit number representation?)

^[.!?]$
SENT tag

^[,]$
Comma

^[Ww][Hh]
Words started with Wh-/wh-/WH-/wH-

^[:;-]$
Colons, hyphens, etc.

^\'[Ss]$
Possessive/tobe

^[\'\"]$
Quotation mark / apostrophe

^[A-Za-z.]+\.$
Words ending with a dot (possibly an abbreviation)
